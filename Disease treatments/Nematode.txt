Pratylenchus Coffeae and Radopholus similis are types
of nematode endoparasit which is switching-moved. The
life cycle of P. Coffeae is around 45 days while R. similis
is approximately 1 month. Coffee plants that stricken
with this disease will be invisible dwarf and the leaves
will turn yellow and deciduous. Nematode is shown at the
right side of the the image below.
